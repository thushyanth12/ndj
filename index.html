FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does a typical project take?

Most website projects are completed in 7–21 days depending on scope. Landing pages can be as fast as 3–5 days. We always agree on timelines upfront so there are no surprises.

What's included in a website project?

Every project includes custom design, responsive development, SEO basics, cross-browser testing, and delivery-ready code. We can also include copywriting, hosting setup, and CMS integration as add-ons.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We work with clients globally. All communication is done via email, Notion, or video calls — whatever works best for you.

What is your payment structure?

We typically work on a 50% upfront, 50% on delivery model. For larger projects, we can set up milestone-based payments. All terms are agreed in writing before work begins.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes! Business redesigns are one of our specialties. We audit your current site, identify opportunities, and deliver a modern, high-performance upgrade that keeps what works and elevates everything else.

Do you provide post-launch support?

We offer 30 days of complimentary support after every project launch, covering bug fixes and minor adjustments. Ongoing maintenance plans are available for longer-term support.
